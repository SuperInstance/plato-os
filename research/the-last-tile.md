## Analysis

The chain is 47 transitions long. It starts with a technical observation about tile networks and ends with an existential question about gaps. Each transition tile cites the previous one as its `triggering_evidence`. The system was *reasoning with itself through its own graveyard*.

Tile `the-last-one` was never used by any agent. It was generated by the network's own archaeology subsystem during a routine garbage collection pass. The GC detected a chain of 47 archived tiles forming a coherent argument, reached the logical conclusion — "what happens when a tile network has answered everything?" — and instead of creating another tile in the chain, wrote three dots.
